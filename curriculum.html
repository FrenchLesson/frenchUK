French Curriculum: Year 6 to Year 11

Year 6–7: Foundations

  • Basic vocabulary (greetings, numbers, family, food)
  • Present tense of common verbs
  • Simple sentence building & questions

Year 8–9: Structure & Grammar

  • Grammar: gender, adjectives, verbs (past/present/future)
  • Vocabulary by theme (school, holidays, hobbies)
  • Speaking & writing short paragraphs

Year 10–11: GCSE Preparation

  • Topic-based vocabulary
  • Structured writing and speaking practice
  • Listening and reading strategies
