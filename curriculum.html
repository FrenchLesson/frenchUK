Curriculum Overview

A progressive French learning journey from KS2 to GCSE

KS2 & KS3

  • Weekly sessions building key grammar and vocabulary
  • Games, speaking practice, and writing confidence
  • Adapted to students’ age and school curriculum

Pathway to GCSE

  • Transition support from Year 6 upwards
  • Pronunciation, listening & sentence structure
  • Gradual preparation for GCSE vocabulary & formats

Lesson Style

  • Calm, supportive, and student-paced
  • Mix of oral, listening, reading, and grammar
  • Resources tailored to student needs
